Choose Your Own Murder Mystery

Enter the mansion and let your decisions take you on a journey... though you may not make it out alive! Try not to use the Back button. If you perish you can replay the story and discover one of the other many endings. Add your name and ending to the Leaderboard after each time you complete the story. Written by me and planned with Twine. Made with postgreSQL, Ruby, HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript.

Give it a shot, Detective!

Let's Quiz!

A trivia quiz built as part of a group project - play against a friend by sending them a join code. 20 questions - 10 seconds per question. Will you make the top three leaderboard? Made with Ruby on Rails, HTML5, CSS3 (SCSS), Javascript and the Open Trivia Database API.

Take the challenge!

Where [over] the world is the ISS?

A place where you can see what the ISS is up to! It orbits the Earth once every 90 minutes or so. You can track where the ISS is located (lat and lon); which country it is over right now; and that country's flag. You can also see when it will next be over your exact location, and who is aboard the ISS currently. Built with React, and using Open Notify, GeoNames, and CountryFlags.io APIs. Fuelled by a love of space and a desire to learn.

Track the ISS now

Netflix vs Stan: A Tic Tac Toe Battle of the Streaming Services

A basic Tic Tac Toe game where each marker is another reason to give Netflix or Stan your money. Try for a draw, and three wins. Made with HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript. Powered by streaming services.

Battle it out!

12 Days of Steve: A Stranger Things Advent Calendar

For lovers of Stranger Things; Steve Harrington's hair; the 80s; character evolution; Christmas and web development. Unwrap a Steve per day in the lead up to Christmas (or just because). Made in my early days of development with Javascript (jQuery), HTML5, and CSS3 (SCSS).
